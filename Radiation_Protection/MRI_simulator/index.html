Interactive MRI Safety & Imaging Simulator

This simulator combines MRI image formation, patient screening, and ACR-style zone safety principles into one interactive learning tool. It reflects the RT-focused MRI safety workflow, including the four-zone concept, daily screening, device labeling, and the major field-related hazards of static, gradient, and RF exposure.
