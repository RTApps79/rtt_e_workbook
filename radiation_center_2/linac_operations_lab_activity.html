Linac Operations Lab 🛠️

Test Your Linac Knowledge!

Purpose

This lab activity is designed to help you apply your knowledge of Linear Accelerator (Linac) components, daily Quality Assurance (QA) checks, and basic operational safety procedures in simulated scenarios.

Task

You will navigate through three interactive challenges: Linac Component Identification, Daily QA Decision-Making, and Emergency Procedure Response. Read each scenario or question carefully and select the best answer(s).

Criteria for Success & Badges

Your success will be based on correctly identifying components, making appropriate QA judgments, and selecting correct emergency actions. Your total score determines "Linac Operations Proficiency" badge eligibility:

  • Gold: 90-100%
  • Silver: 80-89%
  • Bronze: 70-79%
