Inside the Linear Accelerator

1.1 The Path of the Electron

To create a treatment beam, electrons must be produced, accelerated, and steered. You must know the order of components.

ComponentFunction
Klystron / MagnetronProvides the Microwave Power to accelerate electrons. (Mnemonic: Magnetron = Microwave (Lower energy units). Klystron = High Energy for high MV machines).
CirculatorPrevents microwaves from reflecting back and damaging the Klystron.
WaveguideCopper pipe where electrons surf on microwaves to gain speed.
Bending MagnetTurns the electron beam to point down at the patient and filters energy spectrum.
TargetTungsten target. Electrons hit it -> photons produced. Retracted for electron therapy.
Flattening FilterMakes the forward-peaked beam uniform. Retracted for FFF modes.
