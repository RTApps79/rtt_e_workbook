Module 11: Final Test Strategy & Tactics

How to beat the test: Pacing, Logic, and Avoiding Traps.

1

The "Distractor" Trap

ARRT questions often include medically-true but irrelevant information. Read the last sentence first to find what is being asked.

Tactic: Read the last sentence FIRST. Focus on the exact question.
2

The "Best" Answer (Safety First)

When multiple answers are correct, choose the one that keeps the patient safest and keeps you within legal scope.

3

Math Management

  • 2-Minute Rule: If a math problem takes >2 minutes, flag it and move on.
  • Ballpark It: Estimate before calculating so errors are obvious.
