Lab Worksheet: Calculating Dose at Depth

Part 1: Purpose & Key Formula

This worksheet accompanies the **Lesson on Calculating Dose at Depth**. Its purpose is to solidify your understanding of the direct proportion relationship used in Percent Depth Dose (PDD) calculations. You will manually calculate the dose at various depths using the primary formula from the lesson.

Direct Proportion Formula

Dosedepth / DoseDmax = PDDdepth / PDDDmax

(Remember, PDD at Dmax is always 100%)
