Interactive Scene

Scaffolded terminology map

Choose a track, then explore the vocabulary layer linked to the anatomy icon. The dashboard shifts from general language to oncology and radiation therapy language as learners progress.

Term assembly

Build a term by placing parts in order. The same root may appear in different tracks, so users must infer meaning from context.

Clinical scenarios

Read the clue and infer the correct term. Then explain why it fits, using anatomy, pathology, or treatment logic.

Match and sort

Match terms to definitions, then sort them by category. This reinforces recognition, semantic grouping, and clinical context.
