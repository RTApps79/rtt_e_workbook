How to use this simulator

  • Explorer: inspect terms by track and anatomy scale.
  • Assembly: build terms from word parts by dragging or tapping chips.
  • Scenario: interpret clinical clues and select the best term.
  • Match: match terms to definitions and review categories.
  • Flashcards: flip cards and mark known/review.
  • Quick Check: complete a 10-question mixed practice set.

Progress is saved only in this browser using local storage. No data is sent anywhere.
