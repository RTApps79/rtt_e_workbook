DICOM MPR Alignment Lab
Browser-based teaching prototype for CT/CBCT review, multi-planar reconstruction, couch-shift practice, mouse-based registration, and lightweight 3D maximum-intensity projection.
1 · Load CT image set
Drop a folder/ZIP of de-identified CT DICOM slices here, or choose files.
The same uploaded series becomes the reference CT and simulated moving CBCT with randomized offsets.
2 · Moving-image simulation
After upload, the platform automatically duplicates the reference CT into a moving daily-image volume and applies a hidden random LAT/VRT/LNG plus rotation error. This lets students practice recognizing and correcting setup offsets even with one image set.
Advanced optional: load a second DICOM series as the moving/daily image set.

Integration notes
Mount this page/module from the OIS Imaging button or LINAC image-guidance panel. Emit the approved shifts to your treatment console as {lat, vrt, lng, roll, pitch, yaw}. Compressed transfer syntaxes may need Cornerstone image loader/GDCM later; this prototype directly supports common uncompressed CT files.
