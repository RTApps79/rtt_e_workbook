Understanding Jaws & MLCs

Purpose

To understand how Multi-Leaf Collimators (MLCs) work with primary jaws to shape radiation fields, and to practice setting basic field configurations.

Task

This lab has challenges:
1. Set specific field sizes using jaws.
2. Categorize fields as symmetric/asymmetric.
3. Apply MLC patterns to jaw-defined fields.
Observe the visualizer for all changes.

Criteria for Success & Badges

Successfully complete challenges by accurately setting jaws, categorizing fields, and applying MLCs. Your score determines "MLC & Field Shaping Pro" badge eligibility:

  • Gold: 90-100%
  • Silver: 80-89%
  • Bronze: 70-79%
