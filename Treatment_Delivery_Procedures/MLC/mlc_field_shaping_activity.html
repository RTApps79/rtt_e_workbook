Interactive MLC & Field Shaping Lab

Lab Instructions

Purpose

To build field-shaping skill in three stages: setting symmetric jaw fields, then asymmetric jaw fields, then using the MLC leaves themselves to conform a field around a target while blocking an organ at risk (OAR).

A note on terminology

This lab uses CAX (Central Axis) — not "isocenter" — when describing the center reference point of a 2D field. Isocenter is the 3D point in space the machine rotates around; CAX is the correct term for the central axis line/point you see on a beam's-eye-view field diagram like the one below.

Stages & Badges

  • Stage 1: Symmetric fields (jaws equidistant from CAX)
  • Stage 2: Asymmetric fields (jaws at unequal distances from CAX)
  • Stage 3: Drag individual MLC leaves to conform to a target and block an OAR

Final score determines badge: Gold 90%+, Silver 80%+, Bronze 70%+.
