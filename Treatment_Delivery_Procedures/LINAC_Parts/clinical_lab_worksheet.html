Activities 6 & 7 — Photo and Video Documentation

Capture and caption photos of: main circuit breaker, all emergency-off buttons, the radiation warning sign, the modulator cabinet door (closed), the gantry angle digital display, all motion enable bars, and the room lasers.

Record short narrated videos of: couch rotation (0°→90° and 0°→270°), gantry movement (0/90/180/270°), independent jaw movement (X1/X2/Y1/Y2) via hand pendant, room/field light activation, and EPID/kV imager retraction. Upload per your program's submission platform.
